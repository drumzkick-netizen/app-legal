# RecipeMode Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

RecipeMode respects your privacy.

## Data Collection
RecipeMode does not require accounts or collect personal information.

## Local Data Storage
Recipes, ingredients, and preferences are stored locally on your device.

## Photos and Camera
If you choose to scan a recipe, the app may request access to your camera or photo library.

## Third Party Services
RecipeMode may use AI services to analyze recipe images or text. These services process data temporarily to generate results.

## Data Sharing
RecipeMode does not sell or share personal data.
