RecipeMode Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

RecipeMode respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

Information We Collect

RecipeMode does not require account creation and does not intentionally collect personally identifiable information.

Local Data Storage

Recipes, ingredients, preferences, and related app data may be stored locally on your device.

Camera and Photos

If you choose to scan recipes or import images, RecipeMode may request access to your camera or photo library for that purpose.

AI Processing

RecipeMode may use third-party AI services to analyze recipe images or text and extract recipe information. This processing is limited to providing app functionality.

Data Sharing

RecipeMode does not sell your personal information.

Children's Privacy

RecipeMode is not directed to children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
