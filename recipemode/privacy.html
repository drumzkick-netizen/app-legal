Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

RecipeMode respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the RecipeMode mobile application.

Information We Collect

RecipeMode does not require user accounts and does not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or contact details.

Recipe Data

Recipes that you save in RecipeMode are stored locally on your device. This may include recipe titles, ingredients, instructions, and related information extracted from webpages when you paste recipe links.

Website Links

When you paste a recipe webpage link into RecipeMode, the app retrieves and extracts recipe information from that webpage to display and store within the app. RecipeMode does not track your browsing activity outside of this functionality.

In-App Purchases

RecipeMode offers optional in-app purchases to unlock premium features such as saving unlimited recipes. Payments are processed securely by Apple through the App Store. RecipeMode does not have access to your payment information.

Data Sharing

RecipeMode does not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties.

Children’s Privacy

RecipeMode is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the app after updates indicates acceptance of the revised policy.
